SUSHI SALAD

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 avocado
  • 10-15 imitation crab sticks (you can get these at Lotte)
  • 1/2 cucumber

Procedure:

Cut avocado into medium-thin, medium-long pieces. Then, break the imitation crab sticks into thin strips. (The strips will peel off easily) Then, cut the cucumber into thin strips. Mix all of the ingredients, and then it's done!
